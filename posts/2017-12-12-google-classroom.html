Google Classroom

Introduction

  • https://classroom.google.com/
  • Paperless classes conducted using G suite (drive, forms, docs, etc).
  • Students and Teachers must belong to the same domain www.atria.edu.
  • Teacher creates class and invite students using class code
  • Classroom folder automatically created in teacher & student drives.
  • Teacher can differentiate class of students while assigning work
  • Teacher gets the student summary as progress
  • Student gets to know the teachers feedback
  • Unlimited drive space

Features

  • Automation of manual tasks

Conclusions

I have documented the work flow of each of the activities. And it is applicable to all departments. In fact the registration form, subject allotment form and feedback forms are used by all the departments.

I have experimented attendance form, generated automatically, from the students list for the course "Computer Networks". That will work for core, electives and labs where student groups need to be distinguished.

A prototype of google classroom was created to run the "Computer Networks" course and my experience was good. Where, the teacher can assign questions, quizzes and assignments and track the progress of the students on line.
